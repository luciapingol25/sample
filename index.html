Larawan Pangalan Paglalarawan

Padre Damaso

 A Franciscan priest who is always frank and sharp with his words. He is also very talkative.

Maria Clara

 María Clara is described as a devout Roman Catholic who became the epitome of virtue, "demure and self-effacing,” humorless and prone to fainting. Her traits were further described by Rizal as an "Oriental decoration" with "downcast" eyes and a "pure soul".

Donya Pia Alba

 Doña Pia Alba was the wife of Capitan Tiago and the mother of Maria Clara. A woman of wealth and status, she had her husband buy land in San Diego, greatly expanding their business operations

Tiya Isabel

 Kapitan Tiago's sister who helped raise Maria Clara.

Crisostomo Ibarra

 Ibarra was an earnest and idealistic young man. Influenced by his European education, he sought to improve the country; as part of this, he believed in the power of education to enact reforms and made efforts to establish a school in San Diego to this end.
